My Process

Roadmap

Leadership communicates their organization goals. I align our team with those goals and develop a roadmap using Smartsheet. I do this by working alongside an executive director.

Why Roadmap?

Gather Requirements

Once the roadmap is decided, I start to gather requirements with stakeholders. They attend design meetings where the team creates rapid prototypes using Figma.

Ask Good Questions

Backlog Management

From the requirements, specifications, and designs, I start building and prioritizing a backlog issues in Jira. The issues added have user stories and are placed in epics. These epics appear on the roadmap.

Organize and Begin

User Acceptance Testing

I work with quality assurance using Enterprise Tester to review the work that was completed. Then I schedule user acceptance testing sessions. Sometimes these are simply demos or training on the new feature.

Trust, but Verify

Release Notes

After the feature is released, I write release notes and post them to a Google Site. I also email them to certain distribution groups and individual stakeholders. Release notes describe the feature and the business value it brings.

Have a Voice

Monitor

I measure the value of the product. This can come from direct user engagement, stakeholder interviews, and reviewing Dynatrace log entries.
