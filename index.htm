My Process

Roadmap

Leadership reveals their organization goals. We align our team with those goals and develop a roadmap. We do this by working alongside an executive director.

Why Roadmap?

Gather Requirements

Once the roadmap is decided, we start to gather requirements with stakeholders. They attend design meetings where the team creates rapid prototypes using Figma.

Ask Good Questions

Backlog

From the requirements, specifications, and designs, we start building and prioritizing a backlog. We use Jira to manage our workflow. The tickets need to be well written so that the team does not build the wrong thing.

Organize and Begin

User Acceptance Testing

I work with quality assurance to review the work that was completed. Then I schedule user acceptance testing sessions. Sometimes these are simply demos or training on the new feature.

Trust, but Verify

Release Notes

After the featur is released, I write release notes and post them to an intranet website. I also email them to certain distribution groups and individual stakeholders. Release notes describe the feature and the business value it brings.

Have a Voice

Monitor

I measure our work to determine if we have helped or hurt the business. This can come from direct user engagement, stakeholder interviews, and reviewing log entries such as usage statistics and errors.

Gather Feedback
